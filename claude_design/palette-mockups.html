Palette directions — three mockups

Same composite each time: sidebar, “Start here” row, macro strip, prose link. Typography and layout are held constant so only the palette is being judged. Hover the rows and links.

1 · Cooled Copper — the minimal move
Warm cream goes cool near-white; petrol stays; copper is demoted from every link to active states, pills, and data marks only. Links turn petrol. Smallest diff, breaks the cream-and-terracotta cliché.
